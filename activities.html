Engage in Thrilling Adventures of a Lifetime

Matira Beach

Enjoy a relaxing day with loved ones on the largest popular beach in Bora Bora. There you can enjoy a beautiful panorama of the sunset and what vendores have to offer.

Appropriate for all ages.

The cost is free.

Glass Bottom Boat Tour

Explore Bora Bora's underwater wildlife without getting wet! Upon setting your feet on theheered and comfortable boat, a tour guide will share the history and legends of Bora Bora's famous lagoon.

Appropriate for all ages.

The cost is $49 per person.

Private Water Sport Adventure

Choose water skis, mono ski, wakeboard, or a water tube or two on this thrilling one hour water-sport adventure.

Beginner to expert skill level can participate. Most travelers can participate.

The cost is $124 per boat a maximum of 4 passengers.

Full Day Lagoon Cruise and Snorkeling

On this full-day lagoon cruise, enjoy snorkeling with sting rays, sharks, and other marine life. Take a break with a delicious barbeque lunch on a private island with entertainment and a photo shoot.

Most travelers can participate.

The cost is $140.42 per person.

Whale Watching Tour

Spectate the amazing migrational pattern of the humpback whale. The boat is equipped with a directional hydrophone to listen to their majestic songs. Did we mention you can be in the water, at a safe distance, observing the whales.

Most travelers can participate.

The cost is $158.42 per person.

Mary's Famous Restaurant

Savor a delicious meal and drink at the famous Bloody Mary's Restaurant and Bar. Known for their great atmosphere, signature cocktails, and fresh dishes. Oh, you may spot a celebrity too.

Most travelers can participate.

The cost varies depending on the meal and beverage chosen.

Rest and Relax Comfortably

InterCostinental Le Moana

At InterContinental Le Moana Resort, the day starts with breakfast in your overwater bungalow, delivered on a wooden canoe decorated with exotic flowers. Spend the morning exploring world-class dive sites, jumping straight off your private pontoon into azure waters for a swim, or beachcombing along stunning Matira Beach.

Conrad Bora Bora Nui

Welcome to Conrad Bora Bora Nui, a blissful and luxurious resort where relaxation and unparalleled views await you. Located on a private island - Motu To'opua. Our fully renovated resort boasts a variety of accommodations, from the spacious Lagoon View Suites perfect for families, to the spectacular overwater villas and extravagant Presidential Villas.

Tahiti Yacht Charter

Experience the luxury of private yacht cruising in Tahiti with custom cruise itineraries through Bora Bora, Raiatea, Taha’a and Huahine aboard your own private catamaran. The crew will tailor your sailing experience to your desires, taking into account the type of catamaran, the season and the duration of your cruise. Each is equipped with two kayaks, two floating hammocks, dinghy, snorkeling and fishing gear.

Catamaran Polynésien Sur Le Lagon

Kuttu, Polynesian catamaran, offers you the opportunity to live on the lagoon of Bora Bora. On Kattu, we shower on the deck, connecting with nature. You can bathe in crystal clear waters in the morning and enjoy lazing on the spacious deck. You will discover the most beautiful corners of the lagoon and create unforgettable moments.
